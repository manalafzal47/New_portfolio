Designing Ticket Processing System for Ministry of Transportation

Context
Internship – Ministry of Transportation
Role
UI/UX Designer & Frontend Developer
Tools
Figma, PowerPoint, JavaScript
Timeline
Jan 2025 - May 2025
Team
4 Students

Problem

The manual ticket processing system had a few problems:

  • 7-8 Excel files used manually to track tickets.
  • 3+ hours/week spent formatting PowerPoint newsletters.
  • Manual creation of graphs and charts.
  • Delays in retrieving missing ticket info from developers.

Design Process

We began with minimal requirements and used wireframes to explore creative directions. I proposed a flexible application that allowed users to preview or start new newsletters, emphasized past newsletter browsing, and prioritized accessibility.

Developer Feedback

We presented our prototypes to senior developers. They suggested:

  • Move critical graphs like Defect Root Cause higher on the dashboard
  • Separate SSM and All Streams views
Based on this, I restructured the layout and mimicked the old PowerPoint format.

Iterations

We demoed the app 3 times and refined based on feedback. Major changes included:

  • Added DevOps-based high-visibility ticket tracking
  • Improved homepage clarity and navigation
  • Revised chart colors for contrast
  • Replaced calendar picker with dropdowns

What I Shipped

Final Designs

Learnings & Reflections

  • Responding to feedback from non-designers
  • Prioritizing usability for older users
  • Designing systems that serve both users and developers
  • Learning to own both the vision and execution under ambiguity
